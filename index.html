Projects
During my undergrad, I worked on various projects spanning from aircraft design to machine learning. Few of them are listed below.
AIAA Design Competition 2018-19
I worked on the development of a Short Range Large Capacity Transport airplane demanded by AIAA's 2019-20 RFP. I lead a team of 8 people for this purpose and also worked on Optimization, Aerodynamics, and Stability analysis of the plane.
Bachelor's thesis
As a part of my Bachelor's thesis, I lead a team of three people, working on the prevention of Loss of Control (LOC). We worked on prevention of LOC in the event of Actuator failures. We implemented a Linear Model Predictive Control System for the same. The algorithm was developed on MATLAB and Simulink. F-18 High Alpha Research vehicle was used as a test platform for demonstration.
Research Intern at IIT Bombay
I developed a plugin in Gazebo for simulating the airship dynamics. This work was carried out when I interned at Lighter than Air Systems Laboratory, Indian Insitute of Technology Bombay under the guidance of Dr. Rajkumar Pant.
AIAA Design Competition 2018-19
Developed a 7 seater passenger airplane to increase the connectivity to the small counties.
