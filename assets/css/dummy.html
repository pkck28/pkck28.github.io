AIAA Design Challenge 2019-20

I worked on the development of a Short Range Large Capacity Transport airplane demanded by AIAA's 2019-20 RFP. I lead a team of 8 people for this purpose and also worked on Optimization, Aerodynamics, and Stability analysis of the plane. Click on the read more button for the design report.

Bachelor's Thesis

As a part of my Bachelor's thesis, I lead a team of three people, working on the prevention of Loss of Control (LOC). We worked on prevention of LOC in the event of Actuator failures. We implemented a Linear Model Predictive Control System for the same. The algorithm was developed on MATLAB and Simulink. F-18 High Alpha Research vehicle was used as a test platform for demonstration. Click on read more button for the thesis.
